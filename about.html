Who Are We?

The Next Level Devils are a collaborative team of students who strive to develop solutions both as innovators and professionals while raising awareness of current challenges in the aeronautics and astronautics field. We focus on NASA's annual Micro-g NExT, SUITS, and RASC-AL challenges. As these challenges involve developing thoroughly-conceived solutions to genuine problems NASA faces, they require an interdisciplinary effort, so all are welcome! Because of the nature of the NASA challenges we are based around, most of our recruiting is only possible around the beginning of the semester, but we are looking to expand our horizons into other projects, so feel free to let us know if youâ€™re interested!
