Who Are We?

The Next Level Devils are a collaborative team of students who strive to develop solutions both as innovators and professionals while raising awareness of current challenges in the aeronautics and astronautics field.

We focus on NASA's annual Micro-g NExT, SUITS, and RASC-AL challenges. As these challenges involve developing thoroughly-conceived solutions to genuine problems NASA faces, they require an interdisciplinary effort, so all are welcome!

Because of the nature of the NASA challenges we are based around, most of our recruiting is only possible around the beginning of the semester, but we are looking to expand our horizons into other projects, so feel free to let us know if youâ€™re interested! Teams

Suits

NASA SUITS (Spacesuit User Interface Technologies for Students) challenges students to design and create user interfaces for various assets on the lunar surface.
Micro-G

Micro-g Neutral Buoyancy Experiment Design Teams (Micro-g NExT) challenges undergraduate students to design, build, and test a tool or device that addresses an authentic, current space exploration challenge.

Lunabotics

Lunabotics provides accredited institutions of higher learning students an opportunity to apply the NASA systems engineering process to design and build a prototype Lunar construction robot. This robot would be capable of performing the proposed operations on the Lunar surface.
